  • Include strength training exercises 2-3 times per week
  • Maintain a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables
  • Stay hydrated and aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night

⚕️ Medical Check-ups

  • Schedule regular health screenings based on your age and risk factors
  • Monitor your blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Get vaccinated according to recommended schedules
  • Consult healthcare providers about family medical history
